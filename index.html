About Andolini

Andolini is an Italian menswear brand built on the art of tailoring, fine materials, and understated elegance. Each piece is crafted in Italy to the highest standards and chosen for the modern man who values quality and discretion. With private service and boutiques in Astana and Almaty, we bring the spirit of Italian style to Kazakhstan.
